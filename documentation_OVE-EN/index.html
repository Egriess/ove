Presentation

The site OVE, allows each user to create their own single page without having knowledge in development web. Builder-single page of 6 sections, each editable individually

Installation

The installation is simple, you just need to register.

Put what you want in your single page.

Components

Header

The header of the site remains fixed during the navigation. its title is customizable with the possibility to add its own logo. The menu points to each section of the page in the link to the ink, it does not offer the possibility to add external links or internal additional.

Slider

The section slider allows you to display a carousel in the content customizable, the user can determine if the content if there are images or videos. It is of course possible to mix the display of a photo and then move to a video. it operates 100% of the width of the browser responsive. (provided that the source img/video will be of appropriate size.)

Gallery

It makes it easier to view your photos, projects or want for your future visitors want to come back or is in need of your services.

Testimonial

The testimonial, leaving a comment is selected by the user for the loyalty of the users of services. It allows you to display the last name and first name, among other things, display the avatar of the one who wants to leave a comment.

Map

The map section allows the user to be able to be located by visitors of the website who thought to make contact with him. If the user is a company, for example, the site visitors will be able to directly know or it is. The fields are saved in a database, and may be changed at the will of the one. This will automatically update in a database.
Other than the fields populated, the longitude and latitude are also taken into account in the database, the purpose of which is to load its information dynamically in each page.

Contact form

the contact form allows you to receive emails for requests or information on your activity.

Free text

The section of free Text allows the user to write what he pleases. It can accordingly let go his imagination. Every text is editable, and can convey information more or less important.
